Our
Story

INFINI lies somewhere between creative visions and marble veins, serving as the bridge between human and natural design. Sourcing from around the world, we are in the business of stones — and stories.

As a supplier of premium quality fine stone, INFINI’s mission is to connect North American businesses and consumers with exotic, exclusive, exceptional materials in a way that is respectful of local communities and environments. A family-run company, we have been in the industry for close to three decades and have the experience and expertise to ensure our customers are never between a rock and a hard place.

Our
Foundation

INFINI is a natural extension of Divyashakti Granites (DSG). A family-run business for nearly 30 years, it has become a regional leader in India with international presence. INFINI will continue its legacy of innovation and reinvention in the world around it.
